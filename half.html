The Process

Simple as four steps.

No algorithms, no luck draws. Your creativity is judged by a real human team.

01
Choose a Prize
Browse active competitions. Each has a unique drawing prompt, a fixed entry cap, and a real prize waiting.
02
Draw the Prompt
Use our built-in canvas to create your entry. Any art style counts — express yourself freely.
03
Pay & Submit
Pay a small entry fee via Yoco — secure and instant. Your drawing is locked and sent to our panel.
04
Judges Decide
Our team personally reviews every single entry. The winner gets contacted and the prize is shipped.
